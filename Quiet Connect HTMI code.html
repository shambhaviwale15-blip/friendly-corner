About Us

Friendly Corner is a safe, pressure-free online space where shy individuals, introverts, and those who are deaf or hard of hearing can connect without the stress of face-to-face interactions. Through shared whiteboards, text-based communication, live hangout, and playlist, we make it easy to express ideas, collaborate, and feel included: no cameras, no judgment, just connection.

💡 How It Works
